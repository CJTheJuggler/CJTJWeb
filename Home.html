About Me

Hello there, welcome to the Juggling Zone, I have been doing the circus arts for 10+ years. My specialty's are Juggling, Poi, Fire Arts and Unicycle riding which I hope I get to share with you! There are some pictures of the arts I do that you can look at if you are interested. At the Juggling Zone I have the aim of providing a fun place to be, a community of budding circus artists and a learning space. Contact me through the website or my socials if you have any questions or would like a quote on my workshops, performances and more.
